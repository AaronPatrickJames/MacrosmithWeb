Making life easier for clients since 2007

After 20 years in the electronic document management business, our president and CEO, Grier Yartz founded Macrosmith, Inc., a privately owned company based in Pennsylvania. Macrosmith began by servicing the higher education market, then quickly grew to service the auto industry and municipalities.


​Macrosmith currently has hundreds of clients across the nation, with tens of millions of documents stored in our secure, online repositories. You can trust that we provide an exceptional experience.
