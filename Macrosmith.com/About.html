Technical Details

Where can I access the MacroFile system?
MacroFile can be accessed from any secure web browser on desktops or laptops.
What web browsers work best with the MacroFile system?
The MacroFile system works on all of the main web browsers, including Safari, Internet Explorer, Chrome, and Firefox.
How is my data kept safe and secure?
Your documents are saved to the servers in our data warehouse at our corporate office in State College, PA. Copies of your data are also saved online, on a mirrored server at another location, and on our nightly back up. If data is destroyed, we can restore it within 48 hours.
 Your Account

How does your pricing work?
We offer a fixed cost per month price system. No matter how many documents you scan, the price remains the same.
​ What happens if I want to terminate my contract?
We operate using perpetual contracts, so all we need is 60 days notice of termination. You own your data, and it will be returned to you non-encrypted.
